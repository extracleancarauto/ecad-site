03. WHAT YOUR MAINTENANCE PLAN INCLUDES

By enrolling in the Extra Clean Auto Detailing Maintenance Plan, the Client receives the following benefits with each scheduled service:

• Full Tier 1 Clean service at the discounted Maintenance Price (10% savings off standard pricing).

• Reminder notification sent via WhatsApp three (3) days before each scheduled service.

• Priority scheduling — Maintenance Plan clients receive first access to available time slots, particularly during peak demand periods.

• Complimentary tyre dressing and air freshener with every maintenance service.

• Flexible rescheduling within the same week of any scheduled service with 24 hours notice.

• Service history tracking — the Service Provider will maintain records of all work performed on the vehicle for future reference.

04. WHAT’S NOT INCLUDED

The Maintenance Plan covers Tier 1 Full Clean service only. The following services are available separately at standard pricing:

• Tier 2 Deep Clean and Tier 3 Showroom Detail services — available as upgrades at standard rates with applicable maintenance plan member discounts.

• Add-on services such as clay bar decontamination, ceramic sealant application, paint correction, headlight restoration, engine bay cleaning, water spot removal, and odour treatment.

• Repair work including dent removal, paint repair, mechanical service, electrical service, and any non-cleaning related work.

• Coverage for vehicles not specified in this Agreement. Each vehicle requires its own Maintenance Plan enrollment.

05. SCHEDULING &** SERVICE FREQUENCY**

5.1 Service Cadence

The Service Provider will reach out to the Client at the agreed frequency interval to schedule each maintenance service. The standard cadence is every five (5) weeks unless otherwise specified above.

5.2 Rescheduling

The Client may reschedule any individual service appointment with at least twenty-four (24) hours notice without penalty. Rescheduling within twenty-four hours may attract a small inconvenience fee equal to ten percent (10%) of the service price, at the Service Provider’s discretion.

5.3 Missed Appointments

Appointments missed without notice (no-shows) may attract a fee equal to twenty-five percent (25%) of the service price, payable before the next scheduled service.

5.4 Weather Disruptions

Services may be rescheduled by the Service Provider in cases of severe weather, hurricane warnings, or other safety-affecting conditions. Affected services will be rescheduled at no additional cost to the Client.

06. PAYMENT TERMS

Payment is due in full upon completion of each individual service. The Maintenance Plan does not require advance payment for the full plan period. Accepted payment methods include cash, bank transfer, and approved mobile payment platforms. Each service is treated as an individual transaction at the agreed Maintenance Price.

07. PRICING CHANGES

The Service Provider reserves the right to adjust Maintenance Plan pricing with thirty (30) days written notice to the Client via WhatsApp or email. The Client retains the right to discontinue the Maintenance Plan at any time if pricing changes are unacceptable, with no penalty for doing so. Existing maintenance plan discounts will continue to apply to any new pricing.

08. CANCELLATION

8.1 Client Cancellation

The Client may cancel the Maintenance Plan at any time by providing seven (7) days written notice via WhatsApp or email. No cancellation fee applies. Services already completed will not be refunded.

8.2 Service Provider Cancellation

The Service Provider reserves the right to terminate the Maintenance Plan with thirty (30) days written notice to the Client. Reasons may include but are not limited to: relocation outside the service area, repeated rescheduling beyond reasonable accommodation, payment disputes, or any conduct that violates the Standard Service Agreement.

09. QUALITY STANDARD &** CONCERNS**

Each maintenance service will be performed to the documented standard of the Tier 1 Full Clean service. Any concerns regarding service quality should be raised within twenty-four (24) hours of service completion to allow for prompt resolution. The Service Provider commits to addressing legitimate quality concerns at no additional charge.

10. RELATIONSHIP TO STANDARD SERVICE AGREEMENT

This Maintenance Plan Agreement operates alongside the Service Provider’s Standard Service Agreement. The terms of the Standard Service Agreement — including provisions regarding pre-existing damage, limitations of liability, and the right to refuse service — apply to each individual service performed under this Maintenance Plan. By signing this Agreement, the Client confirms understanding and acceptance of those terms.
