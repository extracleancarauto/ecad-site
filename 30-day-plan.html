HOW TO USE THIS DOCUMENT

This isn’t a planning document. This is an execution document. Every item below is concrete, specific, and completable. The discipline is in actually doing them, not understanding them.

THE FOUR-WEEK STRUCTURE

• Week 1: Foundation setup — the structural pieces that enable everything else

• Week 2: Visibility activation — getting the business actually seen

• Week 3: Operational discipline — building the daily habits

• Week 4: Sales engine — actively generating bookings

HOW TO ACTUALLY USE IT

• Print this document so you have a physical copy

• Check each item with a pen as you complete it

• Don’t skip ahead — week 2 builds on week 1, week 3 on week 2

• If you can’t complete a week’s items, extend the timeline rather than skipping

• At the end of 30 days, evaluate honestly what you completed

THE MINDSET
These 30 days determine whether the comprehensive plan becomes real or stays theoretical. Most of these tasks take less than an hour. Some take 15 minutes. The barrier isn‘t difficulty — it’s discipline.

Section 01

Week 1 — Foundation Setup

The structural pieces that make everything else possible.

Day 1 Banking &** business identity**

☐ Open a separate personal bank account dedicated exclusively to business income and expenses

☐ Transfer any existing business cash into the new account

☐ Set up online banking for that account

☐ Save the account number in your phone for client transfers

Day 2 Digital presence audit

☐ Set up Google Business Profile under ‘Extra Clean Auto Detailing’

☐ Verify your business address (or service area for mobile)

☐ Add at least 5 photos of your best work

☐ Add accurate hours of operation

☐ Set up Instagram business account if not already done

☐ Update WhatsApp Business profile with logo, business name, and service description

Day 3 Documentation infrastructure

☐ Create a simple client tracking spreadsheet (Google Sheets is fine)

☐ Columns to include: Name, Phone, Vehicle, Date, Service, Price, Payment Method, Notes

☐ Create a folder structure on your phone or cloud for job photos

☐ Set up automatic photo backup to cloud storage

☐ Print 5 copies of the service contract template for use this week

Day 4 Equipment &** supply readiness**

☐ Inventory all chemicals and consumables in your kit

☐ Identify what’s running low and place orders to restock

☐ Test both pressure washers — confirm both are operational

☐ Pre-mix and label spray bottles at correct dilutions

☐ Organize microfibers by purpose — wash, dry, glass, interior, applicator

☐ Pack a ‘mobile kit’ so vehicle is ready to go on short notice

Day 5 The Serena preparation (Shuttle sold and Serena acquired — this milestone is complete; use this day for the new vehicle instead)

☐ Detail the Nissan Serena S-Hybrid to showroom condition using your own products

☐ Apply Cerakote ceramic sealant to the entire exterior

☐ Confirm tensioner/belt fix is holding after the Fidelity Motors visit — no clunking on ISG stop-start re-engagement

☐ Schedule CVT fluid service and AC system assessment once belt work is confirmed resolved

☐ Photograph the Serena in 8 standard angles for the site and social media

Day 6 Personal &** partner alignment**

☐ Have the partner alignment conversation outlined in the addendum

☐ Walk through the comprehensive plan together

☐ Discuss the 5-8 year timeline and what it asks of both of you

☐ Agree on how business hours interact with relationship time

☐ Document your partner’s emergency access to business information

Day 7 Week 1 review

☐ Review what you completed this week vs what you didn’t

☐ Identify any blockers preventing completion

☐ Take a full rest day — no business activity

☐ Spend time with family and faith priorities

Section 02

Week 2 — Visibility Activation

Getting the business actually seen by potential clients.

Day 8 Content production day

☐ Photograph all your existing equipment in good lighting for content

☐ Create 7 ready-to-post WhatsApp Status pieces from existing material

☐ Create 3 Instagram posts ready to schedule for the week

☐ Write 5 caption templates you can adapt for future posts

☐ Save all content in a clearly organized folder

Day 9 First active posting day

☐ Post the first WhatsApp Status piece between 11am–1pm

☐ Post second piece between 6pm–9pm

☐ Post one Instagram piece during peak engagement hours

☐ Update Instagram Story with behind-the-scenes content

☐ Engage with at least 5 other accounts in your niche

Day 10 Direct outreach round 1

☐ Identify one specific office building or business park in Kingston/St. Andrew to target

☐ Visit during lunch hour to observe and assess access

☐ Speak with security or management about working there

☐ Leave 5 business cards with contact information

☐ Note the visit in your tracking spreadsheet for follow-up

Day 11 Continue posting discipline + follow-up

☐ Daily WhatsApp Status post completed

☐ Send follow-up messages to anyone who has previously inquired but not booked

☐ Send ‘thank you for following’ messages to new social media followers

☐ Reach out to 2 friends or family with vehicles asking for a referral

Day 12 Direct outreach round 2

☐ Identify one residential community or apartment complex in your area

☐ Visit and observe access points and vehicle activity

☐ If posting allowed, leave flyers in approved locations

☐ Engage with any residents or security you encounter

☐ Document the location for ongoing development

Day 13 Partnership development

☐ Visit one auto parts store, mechanic, or tyre shop in your area

☐ Introduce yourself and the business

☐ Discuss potential referral arrangements

☐ Leave business cards

☐ Repeat with one more adjacent business if time permits

Day 14 Week 2 review

☐ Review what you completed this week

☐ Count actual posts made vs target

☐ Note any inquiries or interest generated

☐ Take a full rest day

Section 03

Week 3 — Operational Discipline

Building the daily habits that compound into business success.

Day 15 First live job using new systems

☐ Take a service contract on your next job, get it signed before work begins

☐ Take 8-angle before photos following the standard set

☐ Execute the job to your highest standard

☐ Take 8-angle after photos in matching positions and lighting

☐ Walk client around vehicle for completion approval

☐ Update client tracker immediately after the job

☐ Send next-day follow-up message in 24 hours

Day 16 Pricing discipline check

☐ Review your pricing list against what you’ve actually been charging

☐ Identify any clients you’ve been undercharging

☐ Decide pricing strategy moving forward — no more sub-$5,000 jobs with new clients

☐ Update social media bios with clear pricing tier names if comfortable

Day 17 Tier 1 conversion practice

☐ Reach out to 3 previous Tier 0 clients with maintenance plan offer

☐ Practice the upsell conversation from Quick Wash to Full Clean

☐ Track responses in your spreadsheet

☐ Daily WhatsApp Status post completed

Day 18 Insurance investigation

☐ Contact ICWI, Sagicor, or General Accident about general liability insurance

☐ Get quotes for basic coverage suited to mobile detailing operation

☐ Compare options and document costs

☐ Plan timeline for activation when reserves support it

Day 19 Skill development

☐ Watch at least one professional detailing tutorial video

☐ Identify one technique to try on your next job

☐ Practice on the Serena if no client work scheduled

☐ Document what you learned in a notes app

Day 20 Anchor location follow-up

☐ Return to the office building or community from week 2

☐ Check in with security or management on any updates

☐ Replenish business cards if needed

☐ Look for any new opportunities to engage residents or workers

Day 21 Week 3 review

☐ Count jobs completed this week vs previous weeks

☐ Calculate actual revenue earned this week

☐ Review what’s working in marketing efforts

☐ Take a full rest day

Section 04

Week 4 — Sales Engine

Actively generating bookings rather than waiting for them.

Day 22 Maintenance plan launch

☐ Identify your top 3 clients most likely to enroll in maintenance plan

☐ Reach out individually with personalized maintenance plan offer

☐ Explain the 10% recurring discount and convenience benefit

☐ Goal: enroll at least 1 client by end of week

Day 23 Reactivation campaign

☐ List every client you’ve ever served

☐ Identify those who haven’t booked in 60+ days

☐ Send personalized ‘just checking in’ messages with a recent before/after photo

☐ No hard sell — just visibility and care

☐ Track responses for follow-up

Day 24 Referral program launch

☐ Create a simple referral incentive — e.g., $1,000 off next service for any referral that books

☐ Communicate this offer to all existing clients via WhatsApp

☐ Post the offer on Instagram and WhatsApp Status

☐ Track all referrals and credit clients appropriately when they convert

Day 25 Promotional booking offer

☐ Identify any open slots in the next 7 days

☐ Run a 48-hour flash promotion to fill them

☐ Specific offer — first 2 bookings get $1,500 off any Full Clean

☐ Post across all platforms with clear deadline

Day 26 Direct outreach round 3

☐ Visit one new community, office, or business district

☐ Build on lessons from previous outreach attempts

☐ Refine your introduction based on what’s worked

☐ Document new contacts and opportunities

Day 27 Content batch creation

☐ Spend 1 hour creating content for the upcoming week

☐ Edit and prepare 7 days of WhatsApp Status content

☐ Write 3 Instagram captions ready to use

☐ Schedule posts where possible

Day 28 Financial review

☐ Calculate total revenue earned across the 30 days

☐ Calculate total costs spent

☐ Calculate net profit for the period

☐ Review allocation — owner salary, reserves, equipment fund, vehicle fund

☐ Make any necessary deposits to separate funds

Days 29-30 30-day evaluation

☐ Honest assessment of what you actually completed vs planned

☐ Identify the top 3 actions that produced the most value

☐ Identify the top 3 actions that produced the least value

☐ Adjust your approach for the next 30 days based on real data

☐ Schedule the 6-month review date in your calendar

☐ Take 2 full days off to rest before continuing
